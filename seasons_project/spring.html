Spring

Spring in Bigfork consists of evening rain stroms, the fresh air accompanied, and the occational snow storm. During the spring, most high schools around the state partake in sports such as tennis, golf, and track (during a non-COVID year, of course). This is the time of year to have bonfires on the weekends and spend sometime preparing for the busy summer months when many "Snow Birds" return. Eagle Bend is a well-known golf course that offers amazing scenery.
