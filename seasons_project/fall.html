Fall

While the air is cooler and the trees are changing color, Fall is one of Bigfork's best seasons because it's all the seasons in one! It offers temperatures that aren't too hot and not too cold. Fall activites such as pumpkin picking in Kalispell to Fly Fishing in the Flathead River remind us that the year is coming to a close and the holidays are just around the corner. Hunting is a huge deal in the autumn months (September-December).
